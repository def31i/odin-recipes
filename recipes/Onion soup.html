Onion Soup

Pasta dish of Italian origin, made with broad often ruffled noodles and a tomato or white sauce

Ingredients:

  • 6 tablespoons butter
  • 4 large yellow Onions
  • Garlic
  • Sherry
  • Bay leaf

Steps:

  1. Heat butter in large heavy pot over medium high heat
  2. Sprinkle in onions with sugar
  3. Stir in sherry with onion mixture
  4. Transfer onions to slow cooker, pour in beef broth
  5. Cover and cook for 8 hours
