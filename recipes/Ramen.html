Ramen

Pasta dish of Italian origin, made with broad often ruffled noodles and a tomato or white sauce

Ingredients:

  • Vegetable broth
  • Chili oil
  • Fresh ginger root
  • sesame oil
  • sliced onions

Steps:

  1. Combine broth and noodles in medium saucepan
  2. Reduce heat, add soy sauce and chili oil, simmer for 10 minutes
  3. Stir in sesame Oil
  4. Garnish with green onions
