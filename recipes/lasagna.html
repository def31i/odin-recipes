Lasagna

Pasta dish of Italian origin, made with broad often ruffled noodles and a tomato or white sauce

  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar

Steps:

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the Lasagna
  5. Bake the Lasagna
